Pale Blue Origin

Description

Pale Blue Origin Inc. is a Toronto-based non-profit focusing on developing software solutions that promote sustainability and eco-conscious behaviours. Being a fairly new non-profit organization, providing information about the company, their mission and primary product was at the forefront of the project. Also, incorporating Nasa's photo of the Earth that the company was inspired by and named after was a large consideration for the final site.
